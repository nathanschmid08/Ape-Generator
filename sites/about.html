About

The Ape Generator is a fun, privacy-friendly tool that answers a surprisingly important question: What kind of ape do you look like?

Upload a photo of yourself, and the tool will instantly show you your closest ape look-alike — all processed locally, right in your browser. Whether you're here out of curiosity, boredom, or for the laughs, this tool delivers instant entertainment without compromising your privacy.

No data leaves your device. There are no servers, no tracking, and no hidden AI models sending your face to the cloud. Everything happens on your own machine — fast, safe, and just for fun.

Originally created as a lighthearted side project, the Ape Generator mixes humor with privacy-first technology

Because sometimes, all you need is a digital mirror… held up by a monkey.
